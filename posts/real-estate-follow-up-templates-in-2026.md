# Real Estate Follow Up Templates in 2026

Real estate agents spend countless hours crafting follow-up messages to prospects, clients, and contacts. In 2026, with AI integration becoming standard practice, we're seeing a shift toward automated, personalized outreach that's both efficient and effective.

## The Challenge of Consistent Follow-ups

Every real estate agent knows the struggle: you've made contact with a prospect, but keeping them engaged requires consistent follow-up. Without a system, agents often fall into repetitive patterns or spend hours crafting messages from scratch.

The solution? Templates that work within your existing workflow while leveraging AI to personalize content quickly.

## A Practical Approach

Let me show you how to build a practical follow-up system using AI prompts. Here's an example prompt template that generates personalized follow-up messages:

```prompt
You are a real estate agent named Sarah Johnson. Your contact is named Michael Chen, who expressed interest in 3-bedroom homes in the Riverside neighborhood last week.

Create a follow-up message that:
- References his specific interest in Riverside
- Mentions a new listing that matches his criteria (3 beds, $450k)
- Includes a gentle nudge for next steps
- Maintains professional yet friendly tone

Message should be 2-3 sentences maximum.
```

This approach generates messages like:

"Hi Michael, I noticed you're interested in Riverside properties with 3 bedrooms. I just found a great match at $450k that might interest you. Would you like me to send over the details? Thanks for your time."

## The Workflow

My recommended workflow involves these steps:

1. **Capture contact data** - Name, interests, previous conversations
2. **Select appropriate template** - Based on contact stage (lead, interested, client)
3. **Input variables into AI prompt** - Name, property details, timing context
4. **Review and customize** - Add personal touches or adjust tone
5. **Send with tracking**
